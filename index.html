About Us

We Believe Every Child
Deserves an Education

Every person on the earth has the right to basic needs. Yet many people lack access to fundamental things like education. Sikh Aid Global was established in 2002 as a non-profit organisation devoted to serving underprivileged communities — particularly in the rural areas of India — by providing access to health, clean water, and education to improve their quality of life.

We operate a fully transparent model: we only collect donations for verified cases displayed on our website, acting as a direct bridge between donors and beneficiaries.

100% Verified Cases

Every beneficiary is verified and their profile is published. Donors can view, contact, and even meet recipients.

Community Driven

Rooted in Sikh values of Seva (selfless service), we empower communities rather than creating dependency.

Global Reach

With supporters in the UK, USA, and Canada, we deliver impact in India's most underserved rural areas.

What We Do

Our Core Programs

From skill development to schooling and community welfare, our programmes create lasting change in underserved communities.

Skills Training

We organise skill development workshops covering vocational skills through to digital literacy, equipping participants with tools to achieve economic independence.

Education

Education is a superpower that transforms our planet. By giving the next generation access to quality schooling, we unlock their potential and our collective future.

Welfare Aid

Our welfare activities extend to community development projects — housing for Sikligar families, clean water access, healthcare support, and women's empowerment.
